An eastern gray kangaroos hop along on hind legs at great speed which can reach over 35 miles an hour. It can also travel for long distances at 15 miles an hour.

  • Scientific Name: Macropus giganteus
  • Diet: Herbivores
  • Size: Up to 7 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 8 to 10 years

Eastern gray kangaroos are social animal and stays in group called "mobs". They use clucking noises for communication.
