Neuronal Culture and Treatments

RCN (rat cortical neurons) were cultured as described in zheng et al., 2001b. Study was then focused on the influence of BDNF (brain derived neurotrophic factor), gp120 and glutamate.

RCN was treated with BDNF for 2-6 days, also with gp120 or glutamate for 2-6 days using different doses/concentrations. Then effect is assessed using analysis of variance (ANOVA). For data mining, data were collected from high concentration treatments.
